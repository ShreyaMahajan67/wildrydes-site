Q: How do I request a ride?
A: Sign in to your account, enter your pickup and destination locations, and confirm your ride request. A nearby driver will be matched to your request.
Q: How much does it cost?
A: Pricing is based on factors such as distance, time, and demand. You'll see the estimated fare before confirming your ride.
Q: How does it work?
A: Our system matches you with nearby drivers based on proximity and estimated arrival time. Once matched, you can track your driver's location in real-time.
Q: What if I have a complaint?
A: Contact customer support through the app or website. We're available 24/7 to assist you with any issues.
Q: How do I cancel my ride?
A: Tap the "Cancel Ride" button in the app. Cancellation fees may apply depending on timing.
Q: How do I pay for my ride?
A: Add a payment method to your account (credit card, debit card). You'll be automatically charged after completing your ride.
Q: How many passengers can ride?
A: Standard rides accommodate up to 4 passengers. You can request a larger vehicle if needed.
Q: What if I lose an item during my ride?
A: Contact customer support immediately. We'll help coordinate with the driver to retrieve your item.
Q: How do I share my route information?
A: Use the "Share Route" feature in the app to send your trip details and ETA to contacts.
Q: How do I rate my driver?
A: After your ride completes, you can rate your driver and leave feedback in the app.
Q: Why do you charge a service fee?
A: The service fee helps cover platform maintenance, customer support, and operational costs.
