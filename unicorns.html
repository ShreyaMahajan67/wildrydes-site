Wild Rydes has a dedicated team that negotiates with, trains, and attempts to manage the Grey Sisters Taxi. We take great pride in the quality of rydes we offer, and our staff exercises extreme caution when interacting with the Sisters — especially when the Eye is involved.

Once accepted into the service, the Sisters receive premium perks — free eye-polishing cloths, unlimited drachma coffee, and priority lane exemptions from several pantheons. Our philosophy is simple: slightly calmer Sisters = slightly safer rydes.

Meet the immortals you’ll be trusting with your destination.

Deino

(Dread)

Deino has been driving the Grey Sisters Taxi since before roads were invented. She enjoys being the one who holds the Eye “most of the time,” claiming it gives her a “stronger sense of leadership and mildly better aim.” When not aggressively merging into traffic, Deino likes polishing the Eye and yelling at mortals who question her shortcuts.

Enyo

(Horror)

Enyo joined WildRydes after a long and loud career offering unsolicited prophecy interpretations to unsuspecting heroes. Her specialty is “navigation by destiny,” though she considers traffic signs “suggestions.” Enyo entertains passengers with dramatic retellings of battles she absolutely would have won if she hadn’t been busy sharing the Eye.

Pemphredo

(Alarm)

Pemphredo recently became the designated spokesperson of the Grey Sisters Taxi. She handles customer complaints, retrieves the Eye when it’s thrown during arguments, and apologizes after unplanned detours. In her spare time, Pemphredo enjoys labeling snacks in the taxi fridge and reminding her sisters to use the brakes.
